Technical Support Executive

Job Description

We are looking for a Technical Support Executive to provide technical assistance to customers and resolve product-related issues efficiently.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Provide technical support via phone, email, or chat.
  • Troubleshoot hardware and software issues.
  • Guide customers through step-by-step solutions.
  • Document technical problems and resolutions.
  • Escalate complex issues to senior technical teams.
  • Maintain high customer satisfaction levels.

Required Skills

  • Basic knowledge of networking and operating systems.
  • Strong problem-solving ability.
  • Good communication skills.
  • Customer-focused attitude.
