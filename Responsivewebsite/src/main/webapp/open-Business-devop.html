Business Development Executive

Job Description

We are seeking a motivated Business Development Executive to drive company growth by identifying new business opportunities and building client relationships.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Identify and generate new business leads.
  • Build and maintain strong client relationships.
  • Conduct market research to identify opportunities.
  • Prepare and deliver sales presentations.
  • Achieve monthly and quarterly sales targets.
  • Coordinate with marketing and technical teams.

Required Skills

  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills.
  • Strong sales and persuasion ability.
  • Basic knowledge of CRM tools.
  • Goal-oriented mindset.
