HR Intern

Job Description

We are looking for an enthusiastic HR Intern to assist in recruitment, employee engagement, and HR operations.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Assist in screening resumes and scheduling interviews.
  • Maintain employee records and documentation.
  • Support onboarding and orientation activities.
  • Help organize employee engagement programs.
  • Coordinate with different departments for HR needs.

Required Skills

  • Basic knowledge of HR processes.
  • Strong communication skills.
  • Good organizational ability.
  • Willingness to learn and grow.
